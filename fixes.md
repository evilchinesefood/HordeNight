## Tasks

### 1. [CRITICAL] Post-FX chain runs at 2.25x intended pixels until first resize (double-applied DPR)

**Location:** Source/Engine/PostFx.js:11-19
**Problem:** `EffectComposer` given a custom render target takes `_width/_height` from the target's physical pixels but still sets `_pixelRatio` from the renderer; every `addPass` then sizes passes at physical × DPR = logical × 2.25 (at the 1.5 cap). GTAO's half-res becomes 0.75×-res; bloom's 11-target chain reallocates at 2.25× area. Self-heals on the first window resize, so profiling is non-reproducible. Found independently by 2 agents.
**Fix:** `composer.setSize(logicalW, logicalH)` immediately after constructing the composer, before any addPass.
**Verification:** `?debug` fps before vs after on a DPR>1 display; AO/bloom pass cost drops.

### 2. [HIGH] Shadow map re-renders every frame while moving (snap quantum = 1 texel)

**Location:** Source/Engine/Sky.js:240-259
**Problem:** Texel snap is 6.25cm; walking crosses a boundary nearly every frame, so the gated shadow pass (~0.5-1M tris) runs constantly in the dominant play state.
**Fix:** Snap to 16 texels (1m) — still texel-aligned (no shimmer), ~90% fewer shadow passes while walking. Revisit at M3 (moving casters).

### 3. [HIGH] LOD tree cross-dissolve never actually blends

**Location:** Source/World/Vegetation.js:587-609 **[visual]**
**Problem:** (a) Toggling `material.transparent` at runtime without `needsUpdate = true` keeps the stale `OPAQUE`-defined program — surviving fragments output alpha 1.0; (b) `alphaTest: 0.3` discards the whole card once `texelA × opacity < 0.3`, so even a correct program pops at ~30% opacity. The headline dissolve is a hard pop. Found by 2 agents.
**Fix:** On fade start set `transparent = true; needsUpdate = true; alphaTest = 0.02` (uniform-only change); restore everything at fade end. Also seed `g.fade` in the initial-state branch (removes the `??` and a hidden-class split).
**Verification:** Walk across the 93m handoff: impostor card fades smoothly, no pop.

### 4. [HIGH] Every procedural normal map has an inverted green channel (water: inverted red)

**Location:** Source/Engine/Textures.js:386 (heightToNormalCanvas), Textures.js:885 (waterNormalTexture) **[visual]**
**Problem:** With canvas y-down + `flipY = true` upload, correct encoding is red = 128 − k·dx, green = 128 + k·dy. The ground/building generator writes green = 128 − dy (flipped); the water generator writes red = 128 + dx (flipped). The two generators contradict each other, proving at least one wrong in each; the derivation says green for ground, red for water. Affects terrain splats, planks, shingles, stone, barrels, boulders — shingle/stone/plank grooves (horizontal patterns) are lit from the wrong side, and likely contributed to the roof-stripe fight in the last two commits.
**Fix:** Sign-flip green in heightToNormalCanvas and red in waterNormalTexture.
**Verification:** Browser: shingle course ridges brighten on the sun-facing side (sun azimuth 205).

### 5. [HIGH] Pointer-lock retry loop is self-rearming and unbounded

**Location:** Source/Engine/Input.js:20-41
**Problem:** error → retry → `lock()` re-arms `retryArmed` → error → retry, forever, wherever lock is persistently denied (iframes without `allow="pointer-lock"`, permissions policy, Android Chrome).
**Fix:** `lock(isRetry)` — only arm the retry on user-initiated calls. Also clamp `movementX/Y` to ±200 (first-event spike after lock on some Chrome builds).

### 6. [HIGH] Dev menu ships ungated to every player on the live deploy

**Location:** Source/Main.js:86-121 (UNCERTAIN — gating assumed wanted; trivially revertable if the public menu was intentional)
**Problem:** Day/Night/Rain buttons render above the title overlay (z 1000 vs 10) at dev.jdayers.com/horde/. Night is a half-implemented dev state (task 8). Side bugs regardless of gating: "Rain: auto" initial label ignores `?rain`/`?norain`; clicked buttons keep focus so Space on the pause screen re-fires them; the comment says "while unpaused" but it shows while unlocked.
**Fix:** Create the menu only when `?debug`; init label from `weather.force`; `blur()` after click; fix comment.

### 7. [HIGH] World-gen Math.hypot with no early-out — ~2.4M calls, est. 100-250ms of startup

**Location:** Source/Core/Heightfield.js:68 (heightAt site loop), Source/World/Terrain.js:76 (splat loop), Source/Core/SitePaths.js:42 (pathDistance)
**Problem:** >97% of these calls compute an exact distance only to fail a cheap threshold (14m/9m discs around 6 sites cover ~2% of the world). hypot is 5-20× sqrt.
**Fix:** Squared-distance compare first, `Math.sqrt` only inside the branch; pathDistance tracks best² and takes one sqrt at return.
**Verification:** npm test (determinism tests still pass — values identical to 1 ulp).

### 8. [HIGH] Night preset is half-applied global state

**Location:** Source/Engine/Sky.js:227-236, Source/World/Vegetation.js (grass uSunCol), Source/World/Weather.js (baseCol) **[visual]**
**Problem:** `setNight` swaps sky/sun/hemi/ambient but fog stays daylight gray (horizon glows at night), grass tips keep warm sun-through-blades glow, and Weather re-stomps fog color from its day baseline every frame.
**Fix (partial — full fix is the deferred M2 mutable-lighting state):** night also updates `weather.baseCol` (fog) and a new `veg.setNight()` (grass sun tint). Light *direction* intentionally stays at the day angle (a below-horizon light would be worse); dev-only surface after task 6.

### 9. [HIGH] Warm fog in-scatter is mathematically dead code since the 55-degree sun

**Location:** Source/Engine/Sky.js:21 (uFogSunDir), Sky.js:62 **[visual]**
**Problem:** `pow(dot(viewDir, sunDir), 24)` with a 55°-elevated sun: fogged geometry sits at/below the horizon, max dot ≈ cos 55° = 0.574, 0.574²⁴ ≈ 1.6e-6. The entire fog-patch surgery + 4 uniforms cloned into every material now multiplies by ~zero; comments describe behavior that no longer exists.
**Fix:** Aim `uFogSunDir` at the *horizontal projection* of the sun direction — the golden lobe sits on the horizon under the sun at any elevation, restoring the effect.
**Verification:** Browser: warm fog band visible looking toward azimuth 205.

### 10. [MEDIUM] Barn loft window floats 0.4m in front of the gable

**Fix:** Center at `D / 2` (embedded through the cap, proud 0.04, matching the cabin window pattern).

### 11. [MEDIUM] Step-band collision re-opens at low frame rates (fast falls pop sideways)

**Location:** Source/Engine/Collision.js, Source/Player/Player.js
